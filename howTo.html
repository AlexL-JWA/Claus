Why Are We Here?

We are here to help you maximise your time by outsourcing individual jobs to our experts. We have writers, artists, designers, accountants, coders, computer geeks and a whole host of talented people available for your short term or long term projects. In short, if you need help, you can find it here.

And the best thing is, you don’t need to use a costly agency or waste time with a middle man. You communicate directly with our expert freelancers, eliminating time wasting and stress. Our fees are absorbed by the freelancer, so the freelancer’s charges are all you pay

WORK AND PAY

Describe your project well – tips for creating
the perfect project post

  • Introduce yourself, freelancers want to know who they are pitching to!
  • Describe your company and what you do.
  • Say which area you need help in (design, creative, accounts etc.)
  • Describe your project.
  • State your time frame.

Fixed price projects and hourlies

You can either post your project as a one off fixed price project or as an hourly. With a fixed price project, you set your budget at the start and the price you have agreed on is the price you pay. We will release funds when the freelancer has reached milestones you have set with us.

With an hourly, you agree to pay the freelancer’s hourly rate for however many hours they work (freelancers will bill you at the end of the week and we will pay them using our secure payment system).

MAIN TIPS

How to decide which person to choose?

We have a whole host of talented people just waiting to help you with your project.

  • Review the applications. Read their profiles and think carefully about whether or not they will be a good fit for you project, your company, for you.
  • You need to be able to get along in order to be able to create a successful team.
  • See how other clients rate the freelancer
  • Ask freelancers questions. Our freelancers are available to answer your questions before you decide to hire them.
  • Shortlist and interview. Interview more than one freelancer and if needed request further interviews.
  • Make the decision and hire.

What kind of work can I get done?

Give your freelancer a definitive and concise specification of work. Make sure you are absolutely clear on what you want out of the project. This will help you to avoid costly mistakes.

You can communicate directly with the freelancer using our secure messaging system. Discuss the project further, share files and set your freelancer up for success. Follow up any questions your freelancer might have and ask questions for yourself.

How do payments work?

Pay your freelancer per hour or per project, whichever you choose. For hourly projects, the freelancer bills you once a week, and we'll send a secure payment to your freelancer. For fixed-price projects, we'll release funds to your freelancer after they meet pre-set milestones.

All payments go through our secure system. Choose from the following billing methods:
